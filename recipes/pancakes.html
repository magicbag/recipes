Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

Delicious pancakes, super simple and easy to make by using food processor.

Ingredients

  • 3 quarter cup buttermilk
  • Half cup all-purpose flour
  • Half cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Half tsp baking soda
  • Half tsp salt

Directions

  1. Combine buttermilk, flour, oats, egg, oil, sugar, vanilla, baking powder, baking soda and salt in blender; puree until smooth.
  2. Heat lightly oiled griddle over medium heat. Pour quarter cupfuls batter onto griddle, cooking until bubbles form, 1-2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on other side, 1-2 minutes.
