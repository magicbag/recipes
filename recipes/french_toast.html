French Toast

Description

This recipe is a bit outside-the-box as it uses flour and produces extra fluffly toast. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Quarter Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Half tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick bread slices

Directions

  1. Measure flour into mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
